    <p><strong>Synopsis</strong></p>
    
    <blockquote>
      <p>Supply chain risks pose an increasing threat towards companies. In this dissertation strategies are developed to mitigate supply chain disruption risks. The foundation for this is created using a systematic literature review, expert interviews and several simulation case studies. The results show several factors influencing the strategy selection.</p>
    </blockquote>
