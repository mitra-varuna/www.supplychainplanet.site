    <p><img src="https://www.inboundlogistics.com/cms//resources.inboundlogistics.com/userfiles/USMexico_main_0119.jpg" /></p>Each year, billions of dollars of goods flow between Mexico and the U.S. While trade between the countries shows no signs of stopping, recent events may alter the flow. These include the USMCA, which still awaits ratification; decisions by the Mexican administration regarding construction of its ports and airports; trade disputes between the U.S. and China; and the Unified Cargo Processing program.
