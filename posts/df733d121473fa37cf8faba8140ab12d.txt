    <p>The S&amp;OP pulse check is a unique survey that provides new and different types of insights for S&amp;OP practitioners. The S&amp;OP pulse check started in 2010 and is this year in its 6th edition.</p>
    <p>Thanks to everybody who has made the 2017  S&amp;OP survey possible. Key insights this year are:</p>
    <p><strong>People &amp; Culture</strong></p>
    <ul>
    <li>Effective S&amp;OP processes see more positive cultural change</li>
    <li><a href="http://www.mentaltoughness.online/" rel="noopener noreferrer" target="_blank">Mental Toughness</a> supports effective S&amp;OP</li>
    <li>Behaviours are not addressed enough in S&amp;OP implementations</li>
    </ul>
    <p><strong>Process &amp; Systems</strong></p>
    <ul>
    <li>Integrating financial planning is now the main action in S&amp;OP</li>
    <li>Managing the product portfolio remains underused</li>
    <li>51% believes there is a lack of S&amp;OP process &amp; system innovation</li>
    <li>64% believes there is a need for more S&amp;OP standards</li>
    <li>People skills &amp; resources is the main roadblock to implement S&amp;OP</li>
    </ul>
